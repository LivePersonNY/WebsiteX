Post Featured Video is a very nifty responsive video plugin that helps your users to see a YouTube or Vimeo video or Custom HTML MP4 video instead of the featured image on the blog and blog detail page. 

Add the URL of YouTube or Vimeo in the specific field or upload video in the backend of the post/page and your user will have the pleasure to watch video in place of featured image.

== Description ==

Post Featured video plugin is highly customizable. You can display video in lightbox, enable autoplay video, enable display mode ie. youtube, Vimeo or upload MP4 video. It can replace the post featured image with a video.

== Features ==
This plugin provides the following features:
	* Option for enables/disable video for post types.
	* Autoplay Video
	* Enable/disable popup/lightbox for video 
	* Display mode for video (YouTube, Vimeo, Custom MP4 Video upload) and much more.

== Installation ==

Installation Steps

Follow any of these below process.
Process - 1
1. Upload the folder to `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

Process - 2
1. Visit your WordPress Administration interface and go to `Plugins -> Add New`
2. Search for `Post Featured Video`, and click `Install Now` below the plugin's name
3. When the installation finished, click `Activate Plugin`


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I add a featured video? =

Once you enabled featured video for the post type, go to the post edit page, there is featured image area (in the classic editor and Featured Video in Gutenberg) for you to look for and set URL or upload a video to display video.



= Where do I get the URLs for my videos? =

For YouTube and Vimeo videos, copy the URL from the browserâ€™s address bar. You may also enter a direct URL to any video hosted on your server. 
For example https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HkuKHwetV6Q&t=39s or 
http://example.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/movie.mp4

= Why my video is not displaying? =

Please make a sure post featured image should have been set and video URL must be valid.

= How to enable YouTube or Video URL? =

Go to the Featured Video menu > General Settings tab and choose display mode.

= Is this plugin have a video autoplay option? =

Yes, Go to the Featured Video menu > General Settings tab and choose settings option

= Is this plugin have a lightbox option?
Yes, Go to the Featured Video menu > General Settings tab and choose settings option.
